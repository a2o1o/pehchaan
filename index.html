Not just public speaking — finding your voice, your story, your future.

Proof of Continuity

Our journey shows Pehchaan isn’t a one-off idea — it’s been built step by step:

2024 – Background research with girls in South Delhi to understand challenges and needs.
Early 2025 – Program design and partnership with NGOs.
July 2025 – Batch 1 pilot with 4 girls in Kalkaji, 3 sessions covering identity, storytelling, and final speech.
Aug 2025 – Impact Report published, with before/after recordings and survey results.

Impact Narrative – Batch 1

In Batch 1, 4 girls participated in three sessions. Outcomes included more confidence, better posture and eye contact, and joy at being heard. Their stories showed transformation that data alone couldn’t capture.

See the Difference

Before: “I would freeze when asked to introduce myself.”
After: “I can share my story without reading.”

What Makes Pehchaan Different

  • Confidence-first, not grammar-first: focus on expression over correction.
  • Story scaffolds, not random topics: prompts like “Who I Am” and “A Moment That Shaped Me” helped authentic storytelling.
  • Measured impact: before/after videos and surveys to show growth.
  • Peer-to-peer mentorship: relatable guidance from students close in age.

Future Direction

Pehchaan is just beginning. Here’s what’s next:

  • Batch 2: Expanding to 8–12 girls with peer mentors and alumni buddies.
  • Showcase Platform: Final speeches to be recorded and shared with families and partners.
  • Mentorship Chain: Past participants step up as peer mentors to guide new cohorts.
